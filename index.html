Hans Zimmer

One of Cinema's Finest Composers

A Brief Introduction

Hans Zimmer is a German music composer. He has composed the film scores for some of cinema's biggest and most memorable movies. These include

  • The Lion King - won best original score (Academy Awards)
  • Gladiator - nominated best original score (Acadamy Awards)
  • The Last Samurai
  • Pirates Of The Caribbean
  • The Dark Knight - Grammy Award
  • Inception - nominated best original score (Acadamy Awards)
  • Interstellar

"If something happened where I couldn't write music anymore, it would kill me. It's not just a job. It's not just a hobby. It's why I get up in the morning."

-- Hans Zimmer
